Wipro National Talent Hunt

Nov 23 2021

Palcement Prep
Wipro is conducting a pan India test called the National talent hunt in 2020 to recruit engineering graduates. It is going to happen this year in the month of October, for registration you can check out Wipro careers page.
If you are in the final year of engineering then being prepared can give an edge over others in the Wipro recruitment drive. To be prepared what you have to do is first carefully understand the Wipro test pattern and make a proper schedule to learn all the topics in the Wipro syllabus then focus on practice. We have reviewed the past five year papers and consolidated all repeated questions in the section-wise practice questions, solve them with the utmost care.
Then move to the placement papers section, we have added 5 Wipro placement papers very much in sync with the latest test patterns. Solve at least 3 papers now, and the rest 2 you can do it one day before the exam.
Remember, its all about preparation and practice. If you can put effort now, I'm sure success will be yours. Good Luck!
